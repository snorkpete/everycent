# Family Type

## Definition

Household setting: `couple` or `single`. Shapes how discretionary money is split and how person names appear in the system.

## Context

For couples, the unallocated budget remainder is split equally between the two people for no-questions-asked discretionary spending. Person names (husband/wife or single_person) are stored in settings.

## Contract

- Values: `couple`, `single`.
- Stored in settings.
- Affects discretionary spending split and display of person names.
