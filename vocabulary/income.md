# Income

## Definition

Money coming into a budget. A named source with an amount, optionally linked to a bank account.

## Context

Incomes are straightforward — they represent salary, side income, or any other money entering the household during a budget period. They're created per budget, and like allocations, they get copied forward when a budget is copied.

## Contract

- Belongs to a budget.
- Has a name, amount, and optional bank account link.
- Total income across all incomes in a budget is a key calculated value — the difference between total income and total allocation is the unallocated (discretionary) amount.
